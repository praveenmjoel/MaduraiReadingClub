Our Story

To make Madurai
read like never before.

We didn't start a book club.
We started a movement.

Born in January 2025, the Madurai Reading Club began with a simple belief — that in a city as ancient and intellectually rich as Madurai, there was a hunger for deeper conversation. For books that challenge. For ideas that linger.

What started as a small gathering of curious minds has grown into a vibrant community of readers who meet, discuss, debate, and discover — one book at a time.

We are not a lecture series. We are not a library. We are a living conversation — shaped by every voice that joins us.

How It Works

01

Vote

Members choose one book from five curated options. Democratic, community-driven, and always surprising.

02

Read

Two weeks to immerse yourself. Read deeply. Mark pages. Let questions form. Let ideas brew.

03

Discuss

Every alternate Sunday, we gather for rich, layered conversation — not summaries, but explorations.

The Experience

Our discussions are designed to go beyond the surface. We explore themes, dissect ideas, share personal reflections, and challenge each other's readings with curiosity — not debate.

Sessions are alive and interactive — with videos that bring context, Kahoot quizzes that spark friendly competition, and live polls that capture the room's pulse.

After an hour of deep discussion, we unwind over networking and high tea — because the best conversations often happen in the in-between moments.

And every attendee leaves with something tangible: a beautiful, custom-designed bookmark — a small piece of the session to carry home.

An Invitation

Meet the Author.

Are you a published author?
We'd love to have you.

The Madurai Reading Club believes the most powerful literary experience is when readers get to speak directly with the mind behind the words. We invite published authors to engage with our community — for an evening of genuine, unhurried conversation.

✓

Open to all published authors — fiction, non-fiction, poetry, regional literature, and beyond.

✓

Community-run event — no fee charged to attendees, and no speaker fee offered. Pure literary exchange.

✓

An authentic, intimate gathering of readers who are genuinely curious about your work and your world.
